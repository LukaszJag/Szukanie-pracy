About the job

We are looking for an experienced Microsoft 365 Migration Support Engineer to join Sii and support a large-scale transition from Google Workspace to Microsoft 365 for our international client. You will contribute to a major cloud transformation initiative, ensuring a smooth and reliable user experience across email, collaboration tools, storage systems, and authentication services.

You will work in a friendly, collaborative environment with access to Sii’s learning platforms, Microsoft partnership resources, certification paths, and long‑term development opportunities.

If you enjoy solving technical challenges, supporting enterprise users, and working in a dynamic migration project — apply and join us!

 Your tasks

    Deliver Level 2 support for users migrating from Google Workspace to Microsoft 365, ensuring smooth transitions across email, storage, and collaboration tools
    Troubleshoot issues related to email migration, file transfers, document conversion, authentication, permissions, and Teams configuration
    Assist users with locating migrated content, resolving access issues, and adapting to new workflows and MFA processes
    Monitor and handle hypercare activities, including daily stand‑ups, channel monitoring, office hours, and reporting on issue trends
    Create and maintain knowledge base materials, runbooks, FAQs, and troubleshooting documentation

Requirements

    At least 4 years of experience in technical support, cloud migrations, or Microsoft 365 administration
    Strong knowledge of Microsoft 365 services, including Exchange Online, SharePoint, OneDrive, Teams, and Azure AD fundamentals
    Hands-on experience with Google Workspace environments and cross-platform migrations
    Ability to diagnose complex issues across authentication, data synchronization, file systems, and user access
    Very good English skills (min. B2), enabling effective communication in an international environment
    Fluent Polish required
    Residing in Poland required
